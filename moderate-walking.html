Moderate Walking Routes

Moderate walking routes are the most difficult routes we'll cover. They may not seem challenging for all users, but we recommend you try a couple and decide for yourself if they suit you. If you decide that the routes are a bit much for your purposes, or struggle with the elevation of the routes, please do have a look through our routes of other difficulties.
